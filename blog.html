Top 10 Luxury Overwater Villas in the Maldives for Honeymooners

February 19, 2025

Discover the epitome of luxury honeymoon experiences with the Maldives' most exclusive overwater villas. From Soneva Jani's Water Retreats featuring private slides and retractable roofs to Gili Lankanfushi's massive Private Reserve, these accommodations offer unparalleled romance and privacy. Top properties like Waldorf Astoria's boat-accessible Stella Maris Villas and Six Senses Laamu's eco-friendly retreats combine sustainability with luxury. Each villa provides direct ocean access, personal butler service, and spectacular views. Peak season runs December to April, with better rates available during shoulder season. Book 6-8 months ahead and request sunset-facing villas for the ultimate honeymoon experience.
